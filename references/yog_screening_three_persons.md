# Negative-Yoga Screening — Three Persons

**Purpose:** Apply the *Negative Yogs* reference (see `negative_yogs.md` / `negative_yogs.json`)
to the three birth charts to refine predictions. This is a **screening**, not a final judgment.

## Methodology & Caveats
- **House system:** whole-sign (house = sign counted from the ascendant).
- **Malefics used:** Saturn, Mars, Rahu, Ketu (Sun counted as marginal).
- **Simplified test:** a negative yoga is flagged when (a) a malefic planet occupies or is
  conjoined with the relevant house/lord **and** (b) that house lord sits in a dusthana (6/8/12).
  The document's full condition also requires *aspects*; **aspect calculation is NOT performed here**,
  so results are conservative/indicative and would tighten with a full aspect check.
- **Data gaps:** Person 1's source data was partial (only a few planets given) → screening incomplete.
- **Known data note:** Person 2's source JSON lists Ketu in the 11th, but by sign (Cancer) Ketu falls
  in the 4th from an Aries lagna. Both readings are noted; neither changes the conclusion.
