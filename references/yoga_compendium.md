## 1. Nabhasa Yogas (Heavenly Yogas)
Four kinds: **Akrti** (diagrammatic), **Sankhya** (numerical), **Asraya** (positional), **Dala** (bilateral).
They operate **throughout life**, irrespective of the running dasha.

### 1b. Sankhya (Numerical) — 7
Based on the **number of Rasis** (signs, not houses) occupied by the 7 planets.
(Some coincide with Akrti → use the Akrti name. Golaka is an exception.)

| # | Yoga | # Signs occupied | Effects |
|---|------|------------------|---------|
| 1 | Vallaki/Vina | 7 (all separate) | Many friends, sweet speech, scripture/music, happy, many servants |
| 2 | Damini | 6 | Helps others, wealthy, cattle, brave, learned, children, gems |
| 3 | Pasa | 5 | Active, talkative, characterless, many servants, ultimately imprisoned |
| 4 | Kedara | 4 | Helpful, agriculturist, truthful, happy, wealthy, changing moods |
| 5 | Sula | 3 | Cruel, terrible, lazy, poor, murderous, brave, noted warrior |
| 6 | Yuga | 2 | Heretic, poor, ostracized, bereft of children/honour/virtue |
| 7 | Golaka | 1 (all crowded in one sign) | Poor, indolent, uneducated, dishonoured, unclean, sad, wretched |

### 1c. Asraya — 3
Based on the **nature of the Rasis** occupied (no Bhava consideration).
If Asraya coincides with Akrti → Akrti wins; if with Sankhya → Asraya wins (except Golaka).

| Yoga | Condition | Effects |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Rajju | All planets in **movable** Rasis | Fond of wandering, wealth in foreign lands, handsome, cruel, roguish |
| Musala | All planets in **fixed** signs | Active, famous, king's favourite, heroic, firm mind, honour/wealth/knowledge |
| Nala | All planets in **dual** Rasis | One limb excess/deficient, hoarded wealth, clever, helps kin, handsome |

### 1d. Dala — 2
Based on **Bhavas** occupied by benefics/malefics (Moon excluded).
| Yoga | Condition | Effects |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Mala/Srak | The 3 benefics (Mercury, Jupiter, Venus) only in **Kendras** | Commands pleasures, vehicles, garments, riches, happiness; handsome; many wives |
| Sarpa | The 3 malefics only in **Kendras** | Cross, cruel, poor, wretched, despondent, drunkard, eats in others' houses |

## 2. Candrayogas (Moon-based)
| Yoga | Condition | Effects |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Adhama (worst) | Moon in a Kendra from Sun (conjoined/4/7/10) | Very low morals, deplorable finances, no wisdom |
| Sama/Madhya | Moon in a Panaphara (3/6/9/12) from Sun | Moderate morals etc. |
| Varistha (best) | Moon in an Apoklima (2/5/8/11) from Sun | Best morals. (Moon in own/great-friend rasi aspected by Jupiter[day]/Venus[night] → very happy/opulent) |
| Candra Adhiyoga | Benefics Mercury+Jupiter+Venus in 6/7/8 from Moon (strong, unafflicted) | Commander/minister/king; long, disease-free; prosperity; victory |
| Lagnadhiyoga | Same as above but from the **Lagna** | Similar to Candra Adhiyoga |
| Sunapha | Any planet except Sun in 2nd from Moon | Prosperous, mighty, virtuous, famous, king/minister |
| Anapha | Any planet except Sun in 12th from Moon | Eloquent, rich, lordly, disease-free, happy, well-dressed |
| Durudhara | Planets in both 2nd & 12th from Moon | Famous speech/wisdom/valour; independence, wealth, generous, leader |
| Kemadruma | None of the above (no planet in 2nd/12th, or 4th/10th from Moon) | Loses wife/friends/houses; poverty/disease; unclean; servitude; roguish. **Nullified** if any non-Sun planet is in a Kendra from Lagna/Moon, or Moon conjoined Mars+, or all planets aspect Moon |

*Planet-in-2nd/12th-from-Moon modifiers:* Mars → energetic/heroic/wealthy/adventurous; Mercury → clever/sweet words/arts; Jupiter → wealthy/religious/happy/honoured by kings; Venus → very rich/enjoys senses; Saturn → enjoys others' wealth/leader of men.

**Supporting wealth yogas (Moon/Lagna):** *Upacaya* — all 4 benefics (Moon, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus) in Upacaya houses (3,6,10,11): immense rich (4), wealthy (3), moderate (2), meagre (1). *Vasumati* — same from Lagna or Moon; lives in own house. *Amala* — benefics in 10th from Lagna/Moon → kingship, wealth, sons, fame. *Subhakartari* — benefics in 12th & 2nd from Lagna → long life, safe, happy, rich. *Papakartari* (malefics there) → short-lived, indigent, unclean, limb-deficient.

## 3. Parivartana (Exchange) Yogas
When lords of two Bhavas exchange houses, each behaves as if in its own house.
There are **66** possible exchanges, split into favourable/unfavourable sets.

| Set | Count | Composition | Effect |
|-----|-------|-------------|--------|
| **Mahayoga** (favourable) | 28 | Lagna lord ↔ 2/4/5/7/9/10/11; 2nd ↔ 4/5/7/9/10/11; 4th ↔ 5/7/9/10/11; 5th ↔ 7/9/10/11; 7th ↔ 9/10/11; 9th ↔ 10/11; 10th ↔ 11th | Blessed by Fortune, very rich, lordly, ornaments, honours from king, good children/vehicles |
| **Dainyayoga** (unfavourable) | 30 | 12th lord ↔ 11 others (11); 6th lord ↔ 10 others (10); 8th lord ↔ 9 others (9) | Fool, reviler, sinful, oppressed by enemies, harsh speech, wavering, interrupted undertakings |
| **Khalayoga** (unfavourable) | 8 | 3rd lord ↔ 1/2/4/5/7/9/10/11 | Goes astray; fluctuating fortune; inconsistent speech/mood |

**Parvatayoga:** lord of Lagna posited; lord of *that* house in a Kendra/Kona in own/exaltation →
king with unimpaired wealth, eternal benefits. **Kahala:** extension (next lord also in Kendra/Kona
own/exaltation) → ever-prospering, noble, cheerful, benevolent, wise, respectable.

**Rajayoga:** lords of 9th & 10th conjoined in an auspicious house → king, pomp/power, praised by virtuous.
**Sankha:** that combination in a good house → king, enjoyer of pleasures, liberal.

### Srinkhala / Shrinkhala Parivarthana / Mukkiraga Parivarthana (Triple Exchange)
*(Source 2.)* An exchange among **three** planets: Graha A in B's house, B in C's, C in A's.
All three become strong as if in their own houses. **Weaker than a 2-planet exchange (~50% power);
exchanges of more than three planets are not counted** (effects too diluted). Operates during the
dasha/antardasha of any of the involved planets.

- *Example (Annamalai Chettiyar, Dhanus Lagna):* Venus in Leo (Sun's sign), Sun in Virgo (Mercury's sign),
  Mercury in Libra (Venus's sign) → triple exchange of the 9th, 10th and 11th lords → educator,
  successful entrepreneur, rich man.
- *Exchange by persuasion:* e.g., Venus in Pisces with Moon, and Jupiter in Moon's sign Cancer →
  exchange-by-persuasion between Jupiter/Venus.

## 4. Mahapurusa Yogas (5)
Formed when a planet is in a **Kendra (from Lagna or Moon) that is its own house or exaltation**.
(1 yoga = fortunate; 2 = equal to a king; 3 = king; 4 = emperor; 5 = greater than an emperor.)

| Yoga | Planet | Effects |
|------|--------|---------|
| Rucaka | Mars | Long face, wealth via prowess, heroic, strong, enemy-destroyer, arrogant, famous, victorious, army commander |
| Bhadra | Mercury | Long-lived, sharp-witted, pure, praised by learned, king, wealthy, eloquent |
| Hamsa | Jupiter | Extolled by virtuous, king, conch/lotus/fish marks, beautiful body, virtuous, sumptuous food |
| Malavya/Malava | Venus | Sturdy, courageous, rich, children/wife/fortune, ever-growing, happy, famous, good vehicles, learned |
| Sasa | Saturn | Popular, strength/servants, king/village head, bad character, other women, enjoys others' wealth, happy |

## 6. The 12 Bhava Yogas (benefic Bhava yogas)
Formed when a Bhava's lord is bright/well-placed in own/exaltation **and** the Bhava is aspected or
occupied by a benefic. Names (one per Bhava 1→12): **Camara, Dhenu, Saurya, Jaladhi, Chatra, Astra,
Kama, Asura, Bhagya, Khyati, Suparijata, Musala.** Effects progress from "steadily prosperous,
famous leader" (Camara) through royal paraphernalia and family happiness to "king performing
auspicious rituals, learned" (Suparijata) and "money with difficulty, humiliated, unsteady" (Musalayoga).
