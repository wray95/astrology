# Negative Yogs (Inauspicious Planetary Combinations)

**Source:** "Negative Yogs in Astrology Explained" (YOG-18092022) by *Kiran Sharma*,
based on the classical text **Phal Deepika**. Retrieved from Scribd document `808367372`
(https://www.scribd.com/document/808367372/YOG-18092022). Uploaded by *ankittanmay*.

This reference is intended as a knowledge base for **accurate predictive screening** of birth charts.

---

## General Principle (विवेचन)

In a nutshell (as stated in the document):

- If the **lord of a house AND the house itself** are **aspected or conjoined by malefics**,
  and those **malefics are also placed in that house**, they produce **negative results**.
- The houses **6, 8, and 12** (the *dusthanas* / malefic houses) reduce auspiciousness when they
  relate to benefic houses or lords.
- **Vice versa:** when a malefic-house lord sits in its **own** malefic house
  (6th lord in 6th, 8th lord in 8th, 12th lord in 12th), the negativity **cancels** and a
  **positive yoga** results (Harsha, Saral, Vimal respectively).

**Natural malefics referenced:** Saturn, Mars, Rahu, Ketu (Sun often counted as a marginal malefic).
**Malefic houses (dusthanas):** 6th, 8th, 12th.

## The Yogas

### 1. Duryog / Daridra Yoga (forms "Avyogi")
- **Condition:** Lagna or Lagnesh is conjoined/aspected by a malefic, **or** Lagnesh is in a malefic house.
- **Effects:** Born in bad company; lacks bodily comforts; may suffer disease or allegations on character; poor or defamed.
- **Note (Mantreshwar):** Only a *powerful* Lagna & Lagnesh can nullify the bad effect.

### 2. Nishvya Yoga
- **Condition:** 2nd house or 2nd lord is aspected/conjoined by malefics **and** 2nd lord is in 6/8/12.
- **Effects:** Owns nothing of its own; lives in poverty; dental & eye problems; harsh language; discomfort; "brainless"; uneducated; property snatched by enemies.

### 3. Mriti Yoga
- **Condition:** 3rd house/lord is aspected/conjoined by malefics **and** 3rd lord is in malefic houses.
- **Effects:** Defeat; lazy; lethargic; wrong deeds; shameless; no happiness from siblings; repents later.

### 4. Kuhu Yoga
- **Condition:** 4th house/lord is aspected/conjoined by malefics **and** 4th lord is in 6/8/12.
- **Effects:** No happiness from mother/friends; deprived of property & vehicles; 4th-house karakas afflicted; lives away from own place.

### 5. Paamar Yoga
- **Condition:** 5th house/lord is aspected/conjoined by malefics **and** 5th lord is in malefic houses.
- **Effects:** Deprived of life's happiness/comforts; liar, fool, cheats others; no progeny pleasure (Santan Sukh) or short-lived children; Pitra Drohi; atheist & anarchist; cares only for own living.

### 6. Harsha Yoga — *POSITIVE (cancellation)*
- **Condition:** 6th lord/house conjoined/aspected by a malefic → **not bad** (minus × minus = plus).
- **Effects:** Full happiness and comfort of wealth, children, friends, etc.

### 7. Dushkriti Yoga
- **Condition:** 7th house/lord is aspected/conjoined by malefics **and** 7th lord is in 6/8/12.
- **Effects:** All discomforts of the 7th house; separation from spouse or sickly/cruel spouse; illicit relationship; discarded by friends/siblings; government punishments; defame; secretory-gland issues.

### 8. Saral Yoga — *POSITIVE (cancellation)*
- **Condition:** 8th lord is in the 8th house → negative impacts nullify.
- **Effects:** Strong intellect; educated; prosperous; wealthy; clean-hearted; famous.
- **Note:** Saturn in 8th → long life; Mars in 8th → injuries/discomforts.

### 9. Nirbhagya Yoga
- **Condition:** 9th house/lord is aspected/conjoined by malefics **and** 9th lord is in 6/8/12.
- **Effects:** Misfortune; disrespect to elders/saints/gurus; loses paternal property on his own.

### 10. Daridra Yoga
- **Condition:** 11th house/lord is aspected/conjoined by malefics **and** 11th lord is in 6/8/12.
- **Effects:** Bears loans; ear problems; deprived of elders' comfort; no appreciation; always at others' service like a servant; bears losses.

### 11. Vimal Yoga — *POSITIVE (cancellation)*
- **Condition:** 12th lord is in the 12th house → negative impacts nullify.
- **Effects:** Comfort; independence; famous for good deeds/qualities; least effort for most return; wealth prospers exponentially.
